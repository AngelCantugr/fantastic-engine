## Tips

- **Test rendering**: ASCII art may look different in different terminals/fonts
- **Use monospace**: Always use monospace fonts
- **Consider width**: Keep diagrams under 80 characters for compatibility
- **Escape carefully**: Remember to escape special characters in strings
- **Use tools**: figlet, toilet, boxes, jp2a for ASCII art generation
